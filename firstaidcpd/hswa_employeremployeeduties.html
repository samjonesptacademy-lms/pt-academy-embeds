Primary Legal Responsibility

Employers hold primary legal responsibility for health and safety within their organisations. Key employer duties relevant to first aid and emergency response include:

  • Specific risks associated with various activities (group exercise, swimming, high-intensity training)

The Health and Safety (First Aid) Regulations 1981 require employers to provide adequate and appropriate first aid equipment, facilities, and personnel based on workplace risk assessments.

  • Workplace size and member numbers
  • Nature of activities and associated risks
  • Distance from emergency medical services
  • Staff working patterns and availability

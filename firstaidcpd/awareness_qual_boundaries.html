First Aid Awareness

  • Understanding common emergencies and their signs/symptoms
  • Recognising when situations require emergency response
  • Following organisational emergency action plans
  • Communicating effectively with emergency services
  • Understanding legal responsibilities and limitations
  • Knowing when to escalate situations beyond personal capability

First Aid Qualification

  • Practical hands-on training in first aid techniques
  • Assessment of competence through practical demonstration
  • Certification from HSE-approved providers
  • Legal authority to act as designated workplace first aider
  • Coverage of specific treatment protocols
  • Regular re-certification (typically every three years)

Expense Optimisation
▼
Ensuring all legitimate allowable expenses are claimed reduces taxable profit. Maintaining thorough records and receipt retention enables maximum lawful expense claims.
Pension Contributions
▼
Contributions to private pensions receive tax relief, reducing income subject to income tax. Self-employed individuals must establish their own pension arrangements as they do not receive employer contributions, but contributions attract tax relief equivalent to the individual's marginal tax rate.
Income Smoothing
▼
Where possible, managing when income is received and expenses are incurred can sometimes optimise tax positions, particularly when income approaches tax band thresholds. However, this must be done within accounting rules and cannot involve deliberate income misreporting.
Professional Advice
▼
Accountants specialising in small businesses and sole traders provide valuable guidance on tax planning, compliance, and optimisation strategies. Accountancy fees are tax-deductible business expenses, and professional advice often generates tax savings exceeding advisory costs.

Cash flow shows actual money movement - what came in and went out of your bank account. This is different from profit because: (1) You might get paid for future sessions (cash in now, but service not delivered), (2) You might pay annual expenses in one month (hits cash flow hard that month), (3) Some expenses like depreciation reduce profit but don't affect cash. Positive cash flow means your bank balance grew - critical for business survival!

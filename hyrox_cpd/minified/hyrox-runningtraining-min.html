Running Training for HYROX

Running development forms HYROX training foundation given 8km total distance

Base Running Development
▼
Easy-paced running (60-70% max heart rate) building aerobic base, comprising 60-70% of total running volume.
Training Sessions
• 30-60 minute steady runs
• Gradual volume progression
• 1-2 easy runs weekly
Tempo Running
▼
Sustained moderate-to-hard pace (75-85% max heart rate) developing lactate threshold.
Training Sessions
• 20-40 minute tempo efforts
• Broken tempos (2 x 15 minutes with brief recovery)
• Tempos following workout station simulation
Interval Training
▼
Structured work-rest intervals developing VO2max and high-intensity capacity.
Training Sessions
• 5-10 x 1km intervals
• 8-12 x 400m intervals
• Fartlek-style variable pace running
HYROX-Specific Running
▼
Running specifically mimicking race patterns and conditions.
Training Sessions
• Running following workout stations
• 1km repeats matching race segments
• Running with accumulated fatigue (later in training sessions after significant work)

HYROX creates a unique cardiovascular stress pattern through repeated moderate-high-moderate intensity cycles, distinct from traditional endurance or HIIT training.
During running segments, heart rates typically settle into moderate intensity zones, rising to high intensity or maximal during stations, briefly recovering during transitions, then climbing again during the next station. This repeated pattern creates unique cardiovascular stress distinct from either sustained moderate intensity or traditional HIIT.

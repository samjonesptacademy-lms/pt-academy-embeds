Long Runs
Weekly long runs building aerobic endurance and mental resilience:
Duration: 60-90 minutes for most athletes
Intensity: Easy conversational pace (60-70% max HR)
Progression: Gradually increase by 5-10 minutes every 2-3 weeks
Frequency: Once weekly, positioned after rest day typically

Division Selection Guidance for Trainers

Recommend Open Division when your client:

  • Is new to HYROX or functional fitness competitions
  • Wants to establish a baseline performance time
  • Has limited experience with sled pushes/pulls or farmers carries
  • Is returning from injury or extended training breaks
  • Prefers to focus on completion rather than competitive placement

Recommend Pro Division when your client:

  • Has completed multiple Open division events successfully
  • Demonstrates solid technical proficiency in all HYROX movements
  • Has a well-established strength and conditioning base
  • Seeks maximum challenge and competitive opportunities
  • Can maintain technique under significant fatigue and heavier loads

HYROX Doubles

Two participants complete the event together, sharing running (each runs 4km total) and splitting work at stations. Available in Men's, Women's, and Mixed categories.

HYROX Relay

Four participants each complete two 1km runs and two workout stations before tagging the next teammate. Available in gender-specific and mixed team options.

Age Group Categories

Standard age groups from under-25 through 70+ in five-year increments enable meaningful competition among similarly aged participants across all divisions.

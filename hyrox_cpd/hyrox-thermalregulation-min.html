Heat Production & Dissipation
The Challenge
The sustained exercise intensity generates substantial metabolic heat that must be dissipated to prevent core temperature reaching dangerous levels. Primary heat dissipation occurs through evaporation of sweat, but high humidity environments reduce evaporation efficiency, and inadequate air movement limits convective cooling.

Hydration Considerations
The Challenge
Balancing hydration needs with practical constraints of HYROX events (limited water access, time cost of drinking, gastric discomfort from drinking while running or performing stations) presents challenges. Athletes must develop personalized hydration strategies through training and racing experience.

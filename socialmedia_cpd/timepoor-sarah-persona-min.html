Content Strategy Implications
For this persona, effective social media content would emphasise time-efficient training methods (e.g., "Complete Full-Body Strength Session in 30 Minutes"), evidence-based approaches supported by research, strategies for maintaining consistency despite busy schedules, injury prevention and recovery optimisation, and integration of fitness into demanding lifestyles.

Experiment Systematically
Test variables methodically for actionable insights
Rather than random experimentation, test specific variables methodically. Systematic testing generates actionable insights that can be reliably applied to future content.
Testing Scenarios
Test whether educational content performs better as carousel posts versus Reels
Compare whether captions with questions generate more comments than statements
Evaluate whether posting morning versus evening impacts reach
Test different CTA styles (direct vs. soft sell) for conversion rates
ðŸ’¡ Key Insight

Adjust Underperforming Elements
Diagnose issues and implement corrections
When metrics indicate consistent underperformance, investigate potential causes and implement corrections. Different metrics require different intervention strategies.
Correction Strategies
Declining reach: Increase posting consistency, use trending audio, incorporate more video content
Low engagement: Improve hooks, add clearer calls-to-action, refine audience targeting
Poor saves/shares: Create more actionable, valuable content worth revisiting
High views but low follows: Strengthen brand presence and value proposition
ðŸ’¡ Key Insight
Distinguish between temporary dips (algorithm changes, seasonal factors) and genuine performance issues requiring strategy adjustment

Track Conversion Metrics
Beyond engagement to business results
Ultimately, social media should generate business results. Track enquiries, consultation bookings, and client acquisitions attributable to social media, calculating approximate return on time invested.
Business Metrics to Track
DM enquiries per week and their conversion rate to consultations
Link clicks to booking pages or lead magnets
New clients citing social media as discovery source
Time invested versus client acquisition cost through social media

This business-focused analysis ensures that engagement optimisation aligns with revenue generation rather than vanity metrics alone

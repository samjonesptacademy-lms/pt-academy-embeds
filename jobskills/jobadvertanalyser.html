Words like "must", "required", "essential" indicate non-negotiable requirements. "Desirable", "preferred", "advantage" suggest nice-to-have but not mandatory. Focus your application on demonstrating required skills, and mention desirable ones as bonuses.

Phrases about team environment, values, or working style reveal facility culture. "Collaborative", "supportive", "professional development" suggest positive culture. "Fast-paced", "high-energy", "competitive" may indicate different working environments. Match these to your preferences and work style.

Some phrases warrant clarification in interviews: "flexible hours" (unpredictable scheduling?), "must be sales-focused" (pressure to hard-sell?), "fast-paced environment" (understaffed?), "wear many hats" (unclear role boundaries?).

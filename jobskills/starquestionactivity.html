What is the STAR Technique?

STAR is a structured method for answering behavioural interview questions with specific, compelling examples from your experience.

S
Situation
Set the context and background
T
Task
Explain the challenge or goal
A
Action
Describe what you specifically did
R
Result
Share the outcome achieved

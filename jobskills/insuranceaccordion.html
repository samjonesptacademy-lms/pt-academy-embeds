Covers claims from third parties (including clients) for injury or property damage caused by training activities. Policy limits typically ¬£6-10 million.

Professional Indemnity Insurance ‚ñº

Covers claims arising from professional advice, programme design, or service delivery alleged to cause client harm or loss. Covers professional negligence allegations.

‚úì What's Covered
  • Programme design negligence claims
  • Professional advice claims
  • Inadequate service allegations

‚úó Without This Cover

Treatment Risk/Therapy Insurance ‚ñº

Specialised coverage for trainers providing soft tissue work, sports massage, or hands-on treatments. Addresses higher risk from manual therapy versus exercise instruction alone.

‚úì What's Covered
  • Sports massage injury claims
  • Soft tissue therapy claims
  • Manual therapy complications
  • Hands-on treatment incidents
  • Therapy-related legal costs
‚úó Without This Cover
  • Manual therapy claims not covered by general liability
  • High-risk activities uninsured
  • Significant personal liability
  • Professional registration at risk
  • Cannot offer therapy services legally
Equipment Insurance ‚ñº

Covers business equipment against theft, damage, or loss. Particularly relevant for mobile trainers or those with significant equipment investments.

‚úì What's Covered
  • Training tools and equipment theft
  • Damage to portable equipment
  • Technology and devices
  • Transit damage to equipment
  • Replacement costs
‚úó Without This Cover
  • Stolen equipment not replaced
  • Service disruption from equipment loss
  • Expensive replacement from pocket
  • Mobile trainers at high risk
  • Reduced client experience during outages
Income Protection Insurance ‚ñº

Provides replacement income if illness or injury prevents working. Particularly important for self-employed trainers. Policies typically pay 50-70% of earnings after waiting periods.

‚úì What's Covered
  • Income replacement if unable to work
  • Illness or injury cover
  • 50-70% of average earnings
  • Continues until return to work
  • Financial security during recovery
‚úó Without This Cover
  • No income during illness/injury
  • Personal savings depleted quickly
  • Unable to cover personal expenses
  • Clients lost to other trainers
  • Financial crisis during recovery
Life Insurance and Critical Illness Cover ‚ñº

Protects dependents and personal financial obligations if serious health events prevent working permanently or long-term. Self-employed lack employer death-in-service benefits.

‚úì What's Covered
  • Dependents protected if you die
  • Mortgage/loan payments covered
  • Critical illness lump sum payment
  • Family financial security
  • Business continuation fund
‚úó Without This Cover
  • Dependents financially unprotected
  • Mortgage/debt obligations remain
  • Family loses primary income
  • Business collapses
  • No employer death benefits exist
